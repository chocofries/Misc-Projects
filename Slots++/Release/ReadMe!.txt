
 Welcome to Slots++! The ultimate console slot machine experience!
 
 To play the game, run the .exe file, and then input numbers for menu navigation and for setting your bets!

 If you want something to spend your hard-earned money on, you can visit the shop and buy an assortment of different themes for the game!
 
 Will you be able to buy the coveted mystery item, and unlock its secrets?

 Try your luck and see...
